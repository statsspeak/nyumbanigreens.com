about us

Rooted in Tradition, Karibu Nyumbani!

At Nyumbani Greens, our commitment lies in the production and preparation of nutrient-rich African Indigenous Vegetables (AIVs) such as Managu, Sagaa, Terere, Kunde, and Mito, alongside staple foods like Githeri, Yellow Beans, and Green Grams.

We prioritize sourcing these ingredients directly from local farmers, ensuring freshness and organic quality. Our dedication extends to offering pre-cooked meals that serve as a convenient and nutritious option for the busy lifestyles of Kenyans.

We are driven by the following key objectives that guide our mission and operations.

01

Access to AIVs (African Indigenous Vegetables)

Making nutritional wellness accessible & convenient

02

Farmer Impact

Supporting farmers through education on best farming practices, fair pay & consistent market access.

03

Youth Employment

Prioritizing employment, skill acquisition, and business education for slum youths
